Welcome to Gossairhat Rajamohan High School

The foundation stone of Gonsairhat Rajamohan High School was laid on 15 January 1952. The school was initially named Gonsairhat Minor English School. In 1950, through a meeting, the educated people of the area formed a working committee of nine members by appointing Dembua Roy as the president and Rajamohan Roy as the secretary. The two wives of Rajamohan Roy, the main pioneer of the school, Sarlabala Roy and Nanibala Roy, jointly donated fifteen bighas of land, Rupkant Roy and Pathuram Roy jointly donated seventy-five decimals of land, and Debaru Roy and Ravikant Roy jointly donated twenty decimals of land in the name of the school. The school was approved as a 2 Class Junior School on 1st January 1953. The school was approved as a 4-Class Junior High School from 1st January 1966. The then headmaster of the school was Nanibhushan Sarkar. The school also had a dormitory. Later, Sudhir Chandra Tagore joined the school as the head teacher. In 1982 the school was upgraded to the secondary level. Then Mantri Ram Roy took charge as the head teacher of the school. In 2002, this school was approved as a higher secondary school. Later, Tapas Kumar Bhadra worked as the head teacher of the school. That small school of that day is standing magnificently with branches spread. Md. Mokhtar Hossain is currently serving as the head teacher of the school. The current student strength of the school is around 1500. There are 22 full time teachers, 11 adjunct teachers, 2 clerks, 2 group D and 1 contractual teacher. In 2022 Central Government Swachh Vidyalaya Award (2021-2022). This school has been honored. Awarded in total four categories at the district level

Our History

  • 1952

    Gossairhat Rajamohan High School was established with the vision of providing quality education to the local community. At that time the school's name was Gossairhat Minor English School.

  • 1966

    The school expanded its facilities and introduced new academic programs and upgraded to class 4.

  • 1982

    The school was upgraded to a Secondary School.

  • 2002

    The school was upgraded to a Higher Secondary School.

  • 2026

    We are excited to celebrate our Platinum Jubilee (75 years).
